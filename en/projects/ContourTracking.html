High-speed tracking for a dynamic sphere using contour detection

In a previous projection mapping system for a spherical object moving in a wide area, the hand that grasps the sphere or the occlusion of the table at the time of bouncing become noise for the detection, and then dynamic projection mapping is not robust.

We propose a detection algorithm based on RANSAC using contour information. The evaluation experiments showed that the algorithm was fast and accurate, such as a small error in shielding up to 50% of the sphere's contour. It can be said that more robust tracking and dynamic projection were realized than before.
